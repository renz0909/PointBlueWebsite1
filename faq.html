Before Moving In

  • What is an integrated microstudio?

    An integrated microstudio is a furnished living space that is thoughtfully maximised to achieve not only an aesthetic appeal, but also efficiency and practicality. Each integrated microstudio has its own bathroom and kitchenette, and is well-ventilated and sound-insulated as much as possible to give you the peace and privacy you deserve.

  • What documentary requirements do I have to submit?

    You will have to submit documents that will prove your identity, employment, and financial capacity to pay the monthly fees. These include, but are not limited to, government-issued or company IDs, employment certificate, most recent payslips, and bank or credit card statements.

  • How can I reserve an integrated microstudio?

    We do not accommodate reservations. A unit will only be held for you once the initial deposit has been made.

  • How can I pay for the initial fees?

    We accept payments through bank deposits and checks.

  • Can I share an integrated microstudio with other people?

    You can share it with just one (1) person, since we only allow a maximum of two (2) occupants per microstudio. Both residents will need to submit their requirements through one application form.

    We do not allow kids and pets in the property. Cooking is also prohibited, unless with the use of microwave ovens and steam-based appliances. We have a set of House Rules, which residents must follow.

Moreover, the common areas of the building are covered by high definition surveillance cameras.

During Stay

  • How do I pay the monthly rent and for the utilities?

    You can pay your monthly rent through banks deposits or post-dated checks. The checks must be submitted upon contract signing during your move-in.

    Electricity bills will be slipped under your door, and you must settle directly with Meralco.

    For water, we will be sending you an email billing every month, and payments must be made via bank deposits or transfers. The water billing is computed based on your water reading consumption.

  • What services can I avail of during my stay?

    We have free bathroom cleanings twice a month. This is optional, and tenants may avail of it by scheduling through the Tenant Service Request Form.

    We understand the joy and importance of being able to cook inside your living space. But as a fire safety measure, we only allow cooking using steam-based electric appliances, or the microwave oven that comes standard with your microstudio.

When Moving Out

  • When do I have to notify you that I will be moving out or will not be renewing our lease contract?

    We ask for confirmation of renewal at least thirty (30) days before the end of lease.

  • When can I get my security deposit back?

    Your security deposit will be returned within thirty (30) banking days, after the necessary deductions have been made, given that no provisions of the contract or policies have been violated, and that you have carried out the full term of your lease. This may include, but is not limited to, electricity charges, water arrears, and unit damages.

  • What happens if I pre-terminate my contract?

    Your security deposit will be forfeited should you pre-terminate your contract.
