Integrated Microstudios

Living Spaces in BGC and Makati with a Focus on Accessibility, Property Management & Design

Accessibility

Beautiful design and excellent service aren't exclusive to upscale communities. We implement our design and management principles in prime locations and offer it to the affordable market in the urban setting of Manila. We build integrated microstudios that are accessible money-wise and location-wise.

Property Management

Our resident hospitality specialists provide quality services to suit your needs. We pair our personal service with free internet connection and an online property management platform designed to ensure timely delivery of tenant needs and services. Rest assured, you will be greeted with a smile and be assisted with urgency at all times.

Efficient Design

Each unit is designed by our in-house team of architects, resulting in spaces that are not only beautiful, but also efficient and practical. Our integrated microstudios are well-ventilated, minimalist, and secluded to give you the peace and privacy you deserve.
